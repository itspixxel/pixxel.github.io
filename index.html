Hi, I'm Pixx!

I am an aspiring game developer, and self-taught artist from Stoke-on-Trent, England, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Games Development at Staffordshire University.

I'm passionate about Gameplay Mechanics Programming, especially Procedural Content Generation, and enjoy leading teams to create small games. I have a love for horror and simulation games, and in my free time, I like to listen to lo-fi music, draw, and practice 3D modeling with Blender. Occasionally, I stream games on Twitch with my friends.

MY SKILLS

Always learning, trying to broaden my horizons. Here's everything I've picked up on the way...

Unreal Engine | Unity

Equipped with the knowledge of Unity and Unreal Engine 5. I can easily navigate the engines and have a good knowledge of the pipelines and APIs. I've made various games with Unity showcased in the Works section and developed tools for Unreal Engine 5.

C++ | C# | Java

Experienced in C++ and C#. Good understanding of Object-Oriented-Programming, Garbage Collection, and Memory Management. Also hold some knowledge of Java from developing mods for Minecraft.

3D Modelling

Extensive experience in Blender. Primarily focused on Hard-Surface Modelling and creating 3D Assets for games. Some experience in Sculpting, Animation, and VFX.

Post-Production

6+ years of experience with Premiere-Pro from freelance work. Experienced in editing in different styles and genres such as cinematics, documentaries, and product showcases. Keen sense of flow and great knowledge of creating custom FX. Some experience with Compositing, and Post-Processing.

Game Design and Art

I've taught myself and built up experience in creating Open-world environments, with a good knowledge of real world weathering; wear and tear. Experienced in Stylized UI/UX Design. I also make traditional and digital art.

Real-World Skills

By participating in various game jams such as GMTK 2021 and Game-Off 2022, I have built up experience in working with and managing small and large-scale teams, working under deadlines, time management, organizational skills, problem solving skills, and a keen eye for perfection.
