Hi, I'm Pixx!

I am an aspiring game developer, programmer, and self-taught artist from Stoke-on-Trent, England. Currently, I am pursuing a BSc. (Hons) degree in Computer Games Development at Staffordshire University, where I excel in Game Mechanics Programming, especially in 3D games. In addition to programming, I have experience in 3D modeling, post-production, time management, problem-solving, and organization, all of which have been honed through personal and student projects.

My passion lies in Gameplay Mechanics Programming, specifically Procedural Content Generation, and I enjoy leading teams to create small games. I have a particular fondness for horror and simulation games, and I am an avid collector of retro items. In my free time, I like to listen to lo-fi music, draw, and practice 3D modeling with Blender. Occasionally, I stream games on Twitch with my friends.
